⚠️ Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read this page carefully before proceeding.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Finance Notes account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support team at: manikantaboddu5@gmail.com
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request - [Your Email/Phone]"
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your registered phone number (if available)
    • Your name as registered in the app
    • A clear statement requesting account deletion
  4. Verification: We may contact you to verify your identity before processing the deletion
  5. Processing time: Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7-14 business days

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your profile data
  • Your Aadhaar verification status
  • Your authentication credentials
  • Your chat messages
  • Your uploaded documents

What Data May Be Retained

Important: Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes:

  • Transaction records: Financial transaction data may be retained as required by law and for business purposes (typically 7 years as per Indian financial regulations)
  • Legal compliance: Data required for legal compliance, dispute resolution, or enforcement of agreements may be retained
  • Anonymized data: Aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you may be retained for business analytics

Before You Delete

Before requesting account deletion, please consider:

  • Download any transaction history or documents you want to keep
  • Ensure you have no pending transactions
  • Note that you will lose access to all your transaction history
  • You will need to create a new account if you want to use the app again in the future

Alternative: Request Partial Data Deletion

If you only want to delete specific data without deleting your entire account, you can:

  • Contact us at manikantaboddu5@gmail.com with your specific request
  • Specify which data you want deleted (e.g., chat messages, documents, etc.)
  • We will review your request and inform you of what can be deleted
